B-Zone emphasizes reality and tries to bring reality into the game. In the real world, there are many examples of people who have changed their sex. Sex change is possible nowadays and as written above, B-Zone brings reality to you, so in-game on the community shop list (/shop) there is the possibility to change your sex.

!!! note "Note"
    Learn how you can buy gold by following the steps on [this page](buy-gold.md).

!!! warning "Warning"
    The gold prices presented on this page are standard. It is possible that in-game products may be cheaper if you make the purchase during a discount period.

## Price and Duration

- The price of sex change is 600 gold and can be purchased from the community shop (/shop).
- The sex change is permanent and irreversible, but you can return to the initial sex by purchasing another change.

## How to Buy?

Follow these steps:

1. Access the community shop using the **/shop** command.
2. Select the following item from the displayed list: **Change sex - 600 Gold**.
3. The server will display some information about the sex change process.
4. After reading the information, press the **Order** button.
5. Immediately after pressing the **Order** button, your sex will be changed from the current one to the opposite.
6. **Congratulations!** You can now use boy or girl skins (/clothes), as applicable.
