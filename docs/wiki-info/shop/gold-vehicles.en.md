- Below you will find a list of all B-Zone RPG server vehicles that can be purchased from the DealerShip using [Gold](buy-gold.md).
- Vehicles purchased with Gold from the DealerShip cannot be sold to other players, only returned to the DealerShip.
- The refund value for vehicles purchased with Gold is listed for each vehicle below and represents 40% of their value.
- You don't need a premium account if you opt to buy such a vehicle, only for premium vehicles.
- Learn how you can buy Gold by following the steps on the [Buy Gold](buy-gold.md) page.

## Legend

- **Dealership Price (Gold)** - the price of the good purchased with Gold in the DealerShip from the shop.
- **Dealership Gold refund** - The amount of money you receive if you sell your car to the DealerShip (/v -> vehicle -> sell).
- **Maximum speed** - The maximum speed the vehicle can reach.
- **Based on** - Names of real cars associated by similarity with those in San Andreas.
- **Number of seats** - The maximum number of players that can enter the vehicle at the same time (including the driver).
- **Price in Gold** - Represents the amount of Gold needed to purchase the vehicle.
- **Tunable** - If the vehicle is tunable, the name of the tuning it belongs to is displayed, otherwise the message "Not tunable" is displayed.
