# Clear Warn

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Warning](../../images/wiki/shop/warning.gif){ width=250" }
</figure>

Are you upset that other players no longer trust you because you received warnings (warns) from the RPG server administrators? No problem, open the community shop (/shop) and get rid of unwanted warnings in a few simple steps.

!!! note "Note"
    Learn how you can buy gold by following the steps on [this page](buy-gold.md).

!!! warning "Warning"
    The gold prices presented on this page are standard. It is possible that in-game products may be cheaper if you make the purchase during a discount period.

## Duration and Price

- The price of clearing one warning received from administrators costs 400 gold.
- Once the payment is made, one warning will be deleted from the game, but its traces (history/log on the website) will remain.

## How to Buy?

Follow these steps:

1. Access the community shop using the **/shop** command.
2. Select the following item from the displayed list: **Clear 1 warn - 400 Gold**.
3. The server will display some information about the warning clearing process.
4. After reading the information, press the **Order** button.
5. Immediately after pressing the **Order** button, the server will delete one warning from your account.
6. **Congratulations!** Players may trust you again.

!!! info "Note"
    The log/history on the website will remain unchanged, even if you purchase this product.
