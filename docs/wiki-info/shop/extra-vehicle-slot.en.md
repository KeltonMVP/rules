# Extra Vehicle Slot

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Garage with many slots](../../images/wiki/shop/garrage_alot_of_slots.jpg){ width=400" }
</figure>

Each player's garage is limited to a maximum of 4 slots, which means that only 4 vehicles can fit in the garage. If you need even more vehicles, expand your garage. The B-Zone community shop helps you expand it, type the /shop command!

!!! note "Note"
    Learn how you can buy gold by following the steps on [this page](buy-gold.md).

!!! warning "Warning"
    The gold prices presented on this page are standard. It is possible that in-game products may be cheaper if you make the purchase during a discount period.

## Duration and Price

- The price of expanding your own garage with one more slot costs 800 gold.
- Once a new slot is purchased, it will remain with you permanently.

## How to Buy?

Follow these steps:

1. Access the community shop using the **/shop** command.
2. Select the following item from the displayed list: **Extra vehicle slot - 800 Gold**.
3. The server will display some information about the slot purchase process.
4. After reading the information, press the **Order** button.
5. Immediately after pressing the **Order** button, your garage will receive an additional slot.
6. **Congratulations!** You can buy more vehicles.
