# Vouchers

Vouchers are a form of payment for players, through certain contributions they make to the community.<br>
Some of the people who receive such payments are **Social Media** members. STAFF members (leaders/helpers/admins 1-5), **DO NOT** receive vouchers directly as a monthly payment.


## General Characteristics
* The voucher has a validity of **maximum 6 months** and upon consumption will provide an amount of Gold based on the value of the respective voucher.
* If the player has vouchers that are about to expire in the next 7 days, he will receive a notification upon login that warns him how many vouchers are about to expire and when the first one expires.
* Vouchers can be sold to other players via the command [*/trade*].
* Vouchers have a finite number for sale via [*/trade*].
    * From the moment it was granted by Kelton on the player's account, it can be sold **MAXIMUM 2 times** from one account to another.
    * Once these 2 trades have been made, the voucher remains permanently on the player's account where it arrived. It cannot be sold further, it must be used to receive Gold in exchange.
* Vouchers that are due to expire in the next 24 hours are colored red to alert the buyer that they will expire soon.


## Available Commands
### /voucher /vouchers /vouchere
* The voucher dialog offers pagination, players can select from the dialog if they have too many vouchers to display on a single page, the "<span style="color: #FF0000; font-weight:bold;">>>></span>" element to navigate to the next page or "<span style="color: #FF0000; font-weight:bold;"><<<</span>" to navigate to the previous page.
* Dialog titles will dynamically display the number of vouchers and the page the player is on out of the maximum number of pages they have.
