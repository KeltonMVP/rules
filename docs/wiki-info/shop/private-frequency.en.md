Keep in touch with friends much easier and support your friendships on the B-Zone RPG server through communication. Purchasing a private frequency gives you the ability to communicate with your friends in a safe place, where you have all the power, you decide who you want to stay on your frequency and who not, with options such as setting a password for the frequency, banning certain members from the frequency, or destroying the frequency if you get bored with it.

## Duration and Price

- The price of a private frequency is 299 gold.
- Once purchased, a private frequency will be valid for 31 days.
- You can extend the lifetime of the private frequency by purchasing the option from **/shop** once more, adding another 31 days to the initial duration.

!!! danger "Remember"
    The price is standard, discounts from certain periods do not apply to this product.

## Specific Commands

### /myfreq

This command displays a dialog where you can find details about your frequency, expiration date, and some frequency options such as setting a password, excluding a member from the frequency, excluding all members, or destroying the frequency.

!!! info "Note"
    Immediately after purchasing a frequency, you must choose your frequency ID.

### /setfreq

Using this command you can set a frequency for your Walkie-Talkie.

_Syntax: /setfreq <Frequency> <Password> (10.0 - 79.9 private, 80.0 - 109.9 public, 0 to turn off)_

### /wt

Represents the abbreviation of the object through which you speak, walkie talkie (radio station). This can be purchased from a 24/7 store. The command only works if you have set a frequency for the walkie talkie using the /setfreq command.

_Example: "/wt message"_

!!! warning "Note"
    The command only works if you have at least level 5.

## How to Buy?

Follow these steps:

1. Access the community shop using the **/shop** command.
2. Select the **Private frequency** option from the displayed list.
3. The server will display some information about the private frequency purchase process.
4. After reading the information, press the **Order** button.
5. Immediately after pressing the **Order** button, you will own a private frequency. To configure it, use the **/myfreq** command.
6. **Congratulations!** You now have a frequency just for you and your friends.

!!! note "Note"
    Learn how you can buy gold by following the steps on [this page](buy-gold.md).
