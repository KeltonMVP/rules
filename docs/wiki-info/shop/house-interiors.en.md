# House Interiors

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Interior](/images/wiki/general/Housee.jpg){ width=400" }
</figure>

Beautify your own home and become the most envied neighbor. The B-Zone community shop (/shop) provides you with approximately 40 different types of interiors that you can purchase for your house.

!!! note "Note"
    Learn how you can buy gold by following the steps on [this page](buy-gold.md).

!!! warning "Warning"
    The gold prices presented on this page are standard. It is possible that in-game products may be cheaper if you make the purchase during a discount period.

## Price and Duration

There are three types of packages:

- **Small interior** - 1400 Gold - There are 6 interiors of this type.
- **Medium interior** - 2000 Gold - There are 24 interiors of this type.
- **Big interior** - 2600 Gold - There are 10 interiors of this type.

The house interior is permanent, but is reset to the standard one when the house is sold to another player.

## Characteristics

- To be able to buy a new interior for the house, you must be outside it.
- When you sell the house, the interior will be reset to the standard one.
- All house tenants must reconnect to the server.

## How to Buy?

Follow these steps:

1. Access the community shop using the **/shop** command.
2. Select one of the following items from the displayed list:
    - **Small house interior - 1400 Gold**
    - **Medium house interior - 2000 Gold**
    - **Big house interior - 2600 Gold**
3. The server will display some information about the house interior change process.
4. After reading the information, if everything is in order, press the **Order** button.
5. Immediately after pressing the **Order** button, the server will display a list of available interiors. Select one and press **Select**.
6. **Congratulations!** You now have a new interior for your house.

!!! warning "Notes"
    - If you somehow chose the wrong interior type, you have the **Back** button available to cancel the interior selection.
    - Once you have chosen an interior from the list and purchased it, **the action is irrevocable**, you can no longer cancel or modify it, so **be careful**.
