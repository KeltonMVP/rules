This job is located in the west of Las Venturas city, right at the extremity. Working at the quarry is dangerous due to the extremely steep and quite narrow road that a worker must travel for each run. Find out more about this job by reading the rest of the article.

## Quarry workers occupation

A worker at the Las Venturas quarry must load various materials from the top of the quarry and transport them on the steep road directly into its heart. One run for skill is represented by a round trip.

## Earnings based on skill for one run

- Skill 1 - Approximately $329 without bonuses.
- Skill 2 - Approximately $347 without bonuses.
- Skill 3 - Approximately $299 without bonuses.
- Skill 4 - Approximately $315 without bonuses.
- Skill 5 - Approximately $404 without bonuses.
- Skill 6 - Approximately $437 without bonuses.
- Skill 7 - Approximately $472 without bonuses.
- Skill 8 - Approximately $502 without bonuses.
- Skill 9 - Approximately $540 without bonuses.
- Skill 10 - Approximately $562 without bonuses.

*The average time to complete a run at skill 1-2 is 123 seconds.*
*The average time to complete a run at skill 3-4 is 92 seconds.*
*The average time to complete a run at skill 5-10 is 76 seconds.*

**Please note:**
The above payment is approximate and does not include premium account bonus or other bonuses active on the account.
Players with premium accounts receive 50% more from the base job payment.

## Runs required to advance in skill

- To advance from skill 1 to skill 2, a worker needs 30 runs.
- To advance from skill 2 to skill 3, a worker needs 60 runs. (90 in total)
- To advance from skill 3 to skill 4, a worker needs 120 runs. (210 in total)
- To advance from skill 4 to skill 5, a worker needs 240 runs. (450 in total)
- To advance from skill 5 to skill 6, a worker needs 200 runs. (650 in total)
- To advance from skill 6 to skill 7, a worker needs 250 runs. (900 in total)
- To advance from skill 7 to skill 8, a worker needs 300 runs. (1200 in total)
- To advance from skill 8 to skill 9, a worker needs 350 runs. (1550 in total)
- To advance from skill 9 to skill 10, a worker needs 350 runs. (1900 in total)

## Specific commands

### /work

This is the specific command for this job, as in the case of many other jobs. Using this command, the server will offer you the appropriate vehicle based on your skill to start working. If you are not close enough to the location established as the mission starting point, the server will guide you to this area by setting a red point on your radar.
