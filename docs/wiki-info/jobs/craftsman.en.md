The job location can be seen in image **#2**. The craftsman job consists of gathering the necessary materials to create carpentry objects, processing and transporting them to the House Upgrade business. Dexterity for creating artisan objects will be gained over time, gaining more experience and being able to craft objects of greater complexity and value.

## General characteristics

* The job is legal, located in San Fierro in the King's area (**near Bank SF**) and you can work here starting from level 5.
* The job has four main activities:
    * renting a chisel, a chainsaw and a protective mask from the Craftsman business (**ID 160**)
    * gathering the necessary materials to create objects (*wood, cotton, gold and silver*)
    * processing the materials to create a finished product
    * delivering the finished object and returning to the initial job location
* The rental is valid for 24 hours and is mandatory to be able to work at the job, the rental prices are as follows:
    * *Chisel* - $1000 for 24 hours
    * *Chainsaw* - $500 for 24 hours
    * *Mask* - $500 for 24 hours
* Materials can be obtained by working at jobs, to be able to earn those materials you will need to have **minimum skill 5** at the following jobs:
    * to obtain *Wood* you will have to work at the Lumberjack job
    * to obtain *Cotton* you will have to work at the Farmer job (**planting Cacti**) and then receive cotton after you harvest them
    * to obtain *Gold* you will have to work at the Miner job
    * to obtain *Silver* you will have to work at the Quarry Worker job
* You can hold a maximum of 5 carpentry objects at the same time, then you will have to sell them if you want to create more. They can also be sold all at once.
* There are four categories of crafts, each category having five objects (*chairs, tables, accessories and doors*).
* The job brings you 20 XP for marathon if it is active and 20 XP for clan.
* After you manage to rent the items specified above, you will have to gather the necessary materials to create that object (*you can see the necessary materials and those you have through the /craft command*). After you gather the necessary materials, return to the job location, use the /craft command and create the desired object by pressing the **CRAFT** button. You will have to deliver the manufactured object to the House Upgrade business (image **#4**), then return to the job location with the check received from the business manager to cash your money.
* You will be able to create artisan objects depending on your skill level as follows:
    * between Skill 1 and Skill 3 you can only process chairs
    * between Skill 4 and Skill 6 you can process chairs and tables
    * between Skill 7 and Skill 8 you can process chairs, tables and accessories
    * between Skill 9 and Skill 10 you can process chairs, tables, accessories and doors
* You can work at this job while having a criminal record (wanted).
* The maximum amount of materials (gold bars/cotton/wood/silver) that the player can collect is 999.
* You can view the amount of materials you have through the commands **/inventory**, **/inventar**, **/backpack** or **/inv** if you have at least skill 3 at this job.

## Obtaining necessary materials

### Wood

You can obtain wood by working at Lumberjack, the wood earnings are as follows:

* Lumberjack Skill 5 - you get 10 wood + random(9)
* Lumberjack Skill 6 - you get 15 wood + random(9)
* Lumberjack Skill 7 - you get 20 wood + random(9)
* Lumberjack Skill 8 - you get 25 wood + random(9)
* Lumberjack Skill 9 - you get 30 wood + random(9)
* Lumberjack Skill 10 - you get 35 wood + random(9)

### Cotton

You can obtain cotton by working at Farmer, the cotton earnings are as follows:

* Farmer Skill 5 - you get 1 cotton + random(9) for each cactus harvested
* A plot can have 16 cacti, for a final gain of 16*(1+random(9)) with the note that random(9) will vary for each cactus harvested.

### Gold

You can obtain gold by working at Miner, the gold bar earnings are as follows:

* Miner Skill 5 - 10 gold bars + random(9)
* Miner Skill 6 - 15 gold bars + random(9)
* Miner Skill 7 - 20 gold bars + random(9)
* Miner Skill 8 - 25 gold bars + random(9)
* Miner Skill 9 - 30 gold bars + random(9)
* Miner Skill 10 - 35 gold bars + random(9)

### Silver

You can obtain silver by working at Quarry Worker, the silver earnings are as follows:

* Quarry Worker Skill 5 - 10 silver + random(9)
* Quarry Worker Skill 6 - 15 silver + random(9)
* Quarry Worker Skill 7 - 20 silver + random(9)
* Quarry Worker Skill 8 - 25 silver + random(9)
* Quarry Worker Skill 9 - 30 silver + random(9)
* Quarry Worker Skill 10 - 35 silver + random(9)

**Note:** **random(9)** - represents a random number from 0 to 9

* To advance from skill 1 to skill 2, a craftsman needs 30 skill points.
