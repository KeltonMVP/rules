The action of this job consists of packaging medicines and delivering them to hospitals, from where you will obtain money and, sometimes, certain medical / addiction kits.

## General Characteristics

* The job is located in Los Santos city in Montgomery area and can be used from level 1.
* By typing the /work command you will start working at the job, being placed in a specially arranged interior.
* You will need to turn on the electric generators in turn, carry some boxes into the packaging room and place them in the machine that packages them. Next you will have the mission to deliver them to a hospital and return back to the medicine factory, at which point you will receive your reward.
* The job brings you 6XP to marathon if it is active and 6XP to clan.
* The job can only be started with a clean record (without having wanted).

## Specific Commands

### /work

This command allows you to start the job.

You need to be near the job location to execute the command. If this is not the case, the server will put a checkpoint on your map, marking the place where you need to go.

### /usekit

This command allows you to use the kits received from the job.

You can hold medical kits that give you 100HP or addiction kits that reduce the addiction level by 30 units.

The command works even during wars.

Kits can be sold to other players through the /trade command.

Depending on the skill held, a player can hold a maximum of between 1 and 10 kits, equivalent to the skill level held.

**Example:**

* Skill 1 - 1 healing kit **OR** 1 addiction kit
* Skill 2 - 2 kits - 1 healing kit **&** 1 addiction kit **OR** 2 healing kits **OR** 2 addiction kits.
