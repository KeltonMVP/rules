The Archeologist job consists of finding artifacts from various specific locations for artifact localization (see images **#3** and **#4**), respectively following cleaning and conservation procedures specified in each archaeologist's unique procedure (see image **#1**), after conserving the artifact it must be delivered to the Egyptian Merchant (see image **#5**).

## General characteristics

- The job is legal, located in Las Venturas city in the Lil' Probe Inn area (**near the National Guard HQ**) and you can work here starting at level 3.
- The job has three main activities:
    - actual artifact localization
    - following the artifact collection and conservation procedure
    - selling it to the Egyptian Merchant
- You can hold a maximum of 10 artifacts at the same time, then you will need to sell them if you want to create more. They can all be sold at once.
- There are four artifact categories, each category having 10 nationalities (*Romanian, Moldovan, Egyptian, Russian, Ukrainian, Tunisian, Greek, Italian, Spanish and Chinese*).
- The job gives you 1 XP for marathon if it is active and 1 XP for clan for each artifact sold.
- You can work at this job while having wanted level.
- You can view the number of artifacts you have through the commands **/inventory**, **/inventar**, **/backpack** or **/inv** if you have at least skill 3 at this job or at the Craftsman job.
- The maximum skill for this job is 10.
- If the archaeologist fails to locate the artifact and collect it within 10 minutes, they will fail to collect it.

## Artifact localization time

The artifact localization time depends on your skill level, the required time is divided as follows:

- **Skill 1** - 19 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 2** - 18 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 3** - 17 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 4** - 16 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 5** - 15 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 6** - 14 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 7** - 13 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 8** - 12 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 9** - 11 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.
- **Skill 10** - 10 seconds are needed to locate an artifact.

## Archaeological procedure attempt limit

The archaeological procedure attempt limit depends on your skill level, the limit is divided as follows:

- **Skill 1** - there is 1 attempt available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 2** - there are 2 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 3** - there are 3 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 4** - there are 4 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 5** - there are 5 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 6** - there are 6 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 7** - there are 7 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 8** - there are 8 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 9** - there are 9 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.
- **Skill 10** - there are 10 attempts available, otherwise the artifact will be destroyed.

## Earnings and particularities by artifact

- **Romanian Artifact**: worth **$350**, grants **1 point** of skill.
- **Moldovan Artifact**: worth **$500**, grants **1 point** of skill.
- **Egyptian Artifact**: worth **$650**, grants **1 point** of skill.
- **Russian Artifact**: worth **$800**, grants **1 point** of skill.
- **Ukrainian Artifact**: worth **$950**, grants **2 points** of skill.
- **Tunisian Artifact**: worth **$1,100**, grants **2 points** of skill.
- **Greek Artifact**: worth **$1,250**, grants **2 points** of skill.
- **Italian Artifact**: worth **$1,400**, grants **2 points** of skill.
- **Spanish Artifact**: worth **$1,550**, grants **3 points** of skill.
- **Chinese Artifact**: worth **$1,700**, grants **3 points** of skill.

**Note:**  
The above amounts represent the base value of the artifact, to this amount an additional **50%** of its value is added if you have a **Premium Account**.  
The new value is determined by the formula: (**base_value** / 2) + **base_value**

## Skill advancement

- Skill 1 to Skill 2: you will need 50 points, for a total of 50 points.
- Skill 2 to Skill 3: you will need 100 points, for a total of 150 points.
- Skill 3 to Skill 4: you will need 200 points, for a total of 350 points.
- Skill 4 to Skill 5: you will need 400 points, for a total of 750 points.
- Skill 5 to Skill 6: you will need 750 points, for a total of 1500 points.
- Skill 6 to Skill 7: you will need 1500 points, for a total of 3000 points.
- Skill 7 to Skill 8: you will need 2000 points, for a total of 5000 points.
- Skill 8 to Skill 9: you will need 3000 points, for a total of 8000 points.
- Skill 9 to Skill 10: you will need 3000 points, for a total of 11000 points.

## Benefits for archaeologists working in San Fierro

- You have a **50%** chance that your artifact will be upgraded to the next level. (Ex: Romanian Artifact to Moldovan).
- At higher skill levels you will have a chance to receive a Skin Fragment:
    - Skill 1-7: 0% chance
    - Skill 8: 1% chance
    - Skill 9: 2% chance
    - Skill 10: 3% chance
