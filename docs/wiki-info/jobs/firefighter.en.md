## General Characteristics
- The Firefighter job is a dynamic job based on emergency interventions, different from classic delivery type jobs.
- Players respond to calls through a dispatch system and participate in various scenarios such as firefighting, gas leak management or rescue missions.


## Skill Progress
- This job is built around 10 skill levels:
    - Skill 2 requires **30** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 3 requires **90** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 4 requires **210** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 5 requires **450** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 6 requires **650** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 7 requires **900** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 8 requires **1200** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 9 requires **1550** Job Skill Points.
    - Skill 10 requires **1900** Job Skill Points.


## Intervention Scenarios
- Each stage unlocks new equipment, more difficult interventions and more advanced mission types.
    - Skill 1 will unlock the **Oil Tanker** scenario.
    - Skill 2 will unlock the **Vehicles on Fire** scenario.
    - Skill 4 will unlock the **Dump Truck Fire** scenario.
    - Skill 6 will unlock the **Building on Fire** scenario.
    - Skill 8 will unlock the **Gas Leak** scenario.
    - Skill 10 will unlock the **Rooftop Rescue** scenario.
- Progression Overview
    - **Oil Tanker** - players must extinguish multiple active fires around a burning tanker.
    - **Fire Truck** - players must respond to burning vehicles and extinguish multiple fires spread across the area.
    - **Truck Fire** - players must quickly manage a smaller, but more profitable, single-target fire.
    - **Building on Fire** - players must first put out the fire from the outside, then enter the building and rescue the horse trapped inside before leaving the area safely.
    - **Gas Leak** - players must repair multiple gas leaks by completing a memory minigame on the valves next to each leak, instead of using the hose.
    - **Rooftop Rescue** - players must reach the indicated area, exit the fire truck, take a ladder from the back of the vehicle, carry it to a predetermined location, place it correctly, climb on it and rescue the animal stuck on the roof.


## Skill-Based Vehicles
- The Firefighter job also introduces the first use of custom vehicles for a job, with each vehicle unlocked matching the player's skill progression and scenario difficulty.
- Each unlocked vehicle comes with its own hose attachment configuration, creating a progression not only in speed and style, but also in the efficiency with which the player can respond to emergencies.
    - Skill 1 - **Firefighter Moonbeam**.
    - Skill 2 - 3 - **Firefighter Rumpo**.
    - Skill 4 - 5 - **Firefighter BF Injection**.
    - Skill 6 - 7 - **Firefighter DFT-30**.
    - Skill 8 - **Firefighter Fire Ladder**.
    - Skill 9 - **Firefighter Securicar**.
    - Skill 10 - **Firefighter Elite Fire Ladder**.


## Hose System
- The hose system is completely tied to the fire truck and works based on how the player positions the vehicle in relation to the fire.
    - **ALT/FIRE** to turn the hose on/off for PC players (**/hose** for mobile players).
    - **ALT** near the refill stations to refill the hose with water for PC players & /refillhose for mobile players.
    - All commands related to this job can be found using the **/jobhelp** command.
- The hose includes:
    - Skill-based water capacity.
    - Skill-based extinguishing damage.
    - Skill-based range improvements.
    - Improved hit detection for a more natural extinguishing experience.
    - Hose **ON/OFF** status displayed in the mission text during fire scenarios.
- The hose retains the percentage of water remaining from the previous intervention, unless the player manually refills it at a station.
- The only time the hose starts completely full is when the player starts work via **/work**.
- The firefighter's water tank scales based on skill level:
    - Skill 1 - **700** water units - Approximately **10** trips until empty.
    - Skill 2 - **900** water units - Approximately **13** trips until empty.
    - Skill 3 - **1100** water units - Approximately **15** trips until empty.
    - Skill 4 - **1300** water units - Approximately **18** trips until empty.
    - Skill 5 - **1400** water units - Approximately **20** trips until empty.
    - Skill 6 - **1500** water units - Approximately **21** runs until empty.
    - Skill 7 - **1700** water units - Approximately **24** runs until empty.
    - Skill 8 - **1900** water units - Approximately **27** trips until empty.
    - Skill 9 - **2100** water units - Approximately **30** trips until empty.
    - Skill 10 - **2200** water units - Approximately **31** trips until empty.
