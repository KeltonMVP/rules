## General characteristics

The car jacker steals public vehicles, personal vehicles, gang vehicles or department vehicles, and then delivers them to a drop-off point in one of the 3 cities.

- There are 3 drop-off points in each city.
- To steal vehicles, you need a driving license.
- You must wait 5 minutes from the delivery of the last car to steal another one. The time is saved even when you leave the server.
- The vehicles you can steal are only those corresponding to the skill you have (see below).
- You have 20 seconds to return to the vehicle if you leave it, otherwise you will lose the run.
- You cannot steal bicycles, boats, planes, helicopters or cars whose keys you own.
- Using the /picklock command automatically gives you wanted 1.
- For stealing a police vehicle, you will automatically receive wanted 3, without the right to deliver.
- Car owners and gang members will receive specific messages when their cars are stolen.
- Mafia members cannot steal vehicles from their own faction.
- You cannot steal your own personal car.
- Owners can use /locatecar to find the thief, and gang members have /stealers available.
- Players who have a premium account receive 50% more at the end of each run.

## Earnings based on skill

- Skill 1 - $978
- Skill 2 - $1070
- Skill 3 - $1200
- Skill 4 - $1380
- Skill 5 - $1659

**Note:**

The payment above is approximate and does not include the premium account bonus or other active bonuses on the account.

Players with a premium account receive 50% more than the base job payment.

## Skill advancement

1. To advance from skill 1 to skill 2, you need to steal 60 vehicles.
2. To advance from skill 2 to skill 3, you need to steal another 60 vehicles. (total: 120)
3. To advance from skill 3 to skill 4, you need to steal another 120 vehicles. (total: 240)
4. To advance from skill 4 to skill 5, you need to steal another 240 vehicles. (total: 480)

## Types of cars that can be stolen, by skill

- At skill 1, players can only steal public vehicles (found on the street; anyone can drive them).
- At skill 2, players can only steal unlocked personal vehicles.
- At skill 3, players can only steal gang vehicles.
- At skill 4, players can only steal locked personal vehicles.
- At skill 5, players can steal vehicles belonging to departments or can choose to steal vehicles available for other skills.

## Specific commands

### /work

This command allows you to start working.

It must be used before stealing or breaking into (/picklock) a vehicle. It is available 5 minutes after completing the previous mission (successfully or not).

**When using the command, you will receive wanted 3 if you have skill 5 and it is used on a police vehicle.**

*Note: You can cancel this action using the /cancel carjacker command*

### /picklock

This command will allow you to open locked personal cars that are not being used by another driver at that moment.

Using the /work command beforehand is required.

**When using the command, you will receive wanted 1.**
