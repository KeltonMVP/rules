# Job Clash

<figure markdown="span">
![Jobs list](/images/wiki/jobs/Jobs_list.png){ width="300" }
<figcaption>#1 Job Clash, marked with CLASH</figcaption>
</figure>

Job Clash is a daily competition between players. Each day, a job becomes a Clash Job, and players compete to complete as many successful runs as possible on that job.

## How do you participate?
Work on your active Clash Job and complete runs normally. Each successful run counts as +1 run towards your daily score.<br>
After each successful run, you will receive a confirmation message showing your total runs for the day.

## Daily Winners and Rewards
- At midnight, the Job Clash ends, the winners are announced, and the rewards are automatically given:
- The 1st place player receives - **$200,000**.
- The 2nd place player receives - **$100,000**.
- The 3rd place player receives - **$50,000**.
- After the rewards are distributed, the leaderboard is reset and a new Clash Job becomes active for the next day.

### Leaderboard
​Use the command [/jobclash] to view the current leaderboard. The leaderboard shows Player Name – Races, sorted from highest to lowest.

## Additional Information
- Job Clash can run at the same time as Job of the Day (both can be active together).
- Only successfully completed races in the active Clash Job count towards the leaderboard. Races from other jobs will not be recorded for Job Clash.
- If two or more players finish with the same number of races, the higher position will be awarded to the older player.
- Winners will receive rewards even if they are offline at midnight.
