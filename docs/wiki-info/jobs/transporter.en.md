## General characteristics

- Players working as couriers must deliver packages to houses indicated by the server.
- The courier job is legal and requires a minimum level of 3.
- You can get hired from the K.A.C.C Military Fuels area, Las Venturas.
- After that, you must deliver the packages to individual houses.

## Package delivery procedure at houses

1. Players will receive a red checkpoint on the map towards the entrance of the house they need to go to.
2. Players should not exit the vehicle or enter the red checkpoint when they arrive at the house, they must wait.
3. Players will need to take the package from the trunk and bring it to the house door, then return to the vehicle.

**Important notes:**

- Your character is automatically removed from the vehicle when you get close enough to the indicated house.
- You have 40 seconds to return to the vehicle after delivering the package, otherwise you will lose the mission.

## Maximum deliveries based on skill and delivery distance

- Skill 1-2: maximum 10 packages, distance between 500 and 750 units.
- Skill 3-4: maximum 20 packages, distance between 750 and 1,000 units.
- Skill 5-6: maximum 30 packages, distance between 1,000 and 1,250 units.
- Skill 7-8: maximum 40 packages, distance between 1,250 and 1,500 units.
- Skill 9-10: maximum 50 packages, distance between 1,500 and 2,000 units.

## Earnings based on skill and delivery distance

- Skill 1-2: 0.5 * **distance_traveled**, approximately between $250 and $375.
- Skill 3-4: 0.7 * **distance_traveled**, approximately between $525 and $700.
- Skill 5-6: 0.9 * **distance_traveled**, approximately between $900 and $1,125
- Skill 7-8: 1.1 * **distance_traveled**, approximately between $1,375 and $1,650.
- Skill 9-10: 1.3 * **distance_traveled**, approximately between $1,950 and $2,600

**Important notes:**

- The amounts above are base amounts, without any other bonuses applied.
- Players with Premium Accounts will receive 50% more money when delivering the package.
- The job also contains a bonus system, which starts at $100 per 10 deliveries and increases by $20 for every 10 consecutive deliveries.
- The maximum bonus can reach up to $10,000.

## Skill advancement

1. To advance from skill 1 to skill 2, you must complete 60 runs.
2. To advance from skill 2 to skill 3, you must complete another 60 runs. (total: 120)
3. To advance from skill 3 to skill 4, you must complete 120 runs. (total: 240)
4. To advance from skill 4 to skill 5, you must complete 240 runs. (total: 480)
5. To advance from skill 5 to skill 6, you must complete 200 runs. (total: 680)
6. To advance from skill 6 to skill 7, you must complete 250 runs. (total: 930)
7. To advance from skill 7 to skill 8, you must complete 300 runs. (total: 1230)
8. To advance from skill 8 to skill 9, you must complete 350 runs. (total: 1580)
9. To advance from skill 9 to skill 10, you must complete 350 runs. (total: 1930)

### Skill points allocation

- Skill 1-4: players will receive 1 skill point per delivery.
- Skill 5-6: players will receive 2 skill points per delivery.
- Skill 7-10: players will receive 3 skill points per delivery.

### Marathon/clan xp points allocation

- Skill 1-4: players will receive 1 marathon/clan xp point per delivery.
- Skill 5-6: players will receive 2 marathon/clan xp points per delivery.
- Skill 7-10: players will receive 4 marathon/clan xp points per delivery

**Important notes:**

- These are the base points you receive, without bonuses from skins.

## Specific commands

### /work

This command allows you to start working.

You need to be near the hiring location to execute the command. If this is not the case, the server will place a checkpoint on the map, marking the location where you need to go.
