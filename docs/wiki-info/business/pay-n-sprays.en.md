In Pay'n'Spray garages, players can repair their damaged vehicles, for a sum of money.

## Characteristics

- The B-Zone RPG server has 10 such locations (4 in Los Santos, 3 in San Fierro, 3 in Las Venturas).
    - **LS:** Dilimore / Temple / Idlewood
    - **LV:** Redsands East / Royal Casino / Fort Carson
    - **SF:** Downtown / Juniper Hollow / El Quebrados
- All garages are marked on the map with a green icon (see image #1).
- Repairs cost $400, regardless of vehicle.
- You must be in front of the "i" at the garage to be able to enter.
- This business does not have a specific interior, apart from the garage interior used for repairs, which is not accessible to pedestrians.
- Each Pay'n'Spray location represents a "Safezone" type area.

## Specific Commands

### /enter

With this command you can repair your vehicle at the price of $200. You must be near the business.

*Note: The command can also be used in other situations, locations or systems.*

*Also, this type of business can be entered using the LALT key.*
