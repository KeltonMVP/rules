B-Zone servers have a total of 3 Sex Shops, one in each of the three major cities.

These businesses have an entertainment role, so the items you can buy from them are strictly for your amusement and that of other players.

## Characteristics

- To be able to buy an item from a Sex Shop, the player will need to go to the indicator inside the business and press the Y key.
- When this key is pressed, a specific interface will open on the player's screen.
- The left/right arrows are used to navigate through the available items and the order button is used to buy an item or cancel to close the interface.
- It should be noted that two such items cannot be held simultaneously.
- This type of business has an interior.
