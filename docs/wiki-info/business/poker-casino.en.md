For those passionate about Texas Hold'em style Poker, B-Zone servers provide such a system located within the Flamingo business in Las Venturas.

The poker tables are located at the end of the interior, waiting for eager players to win.

## Characteristics

- You can play poker if you have a minimum level 3.
- General poker rules apply.
- There can be at most 6 players simultaneously at a single table.
- To be able to join a table you need a minimum level 3 and the minimum amount for entry, which is displayed at each table.
- There are a total of 8 tables, each with its minimum entry amount.
- From the pot won at the end of a round, 95% of the money will be received by the winning player or players, 4.9% of the money will be deleted from the server and 0.1% of the money will go into the business safe.
- This business has an interior.
- Leaving the table is done through the LEAVE button.
- The poker game on the server does not fully comply with classic rules, sometimes having regulatory differences.

## Poker Hand Rankings

### Straight Flush

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Straight Flush](/images/wiki/general/chinta.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Five cards in numerical order, all of the same suit.

### Four of a Kind

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Four of a Kind](/images/wiki/general/careu.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Four cards having the same rank and a fifth additional card or 'kicker'.

### Full House

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Full House](/images/wiki/general/full.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Three cards of the same rank and two cards having another rank, but equal to each other.

### Flush

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Flush](/images/wiki/general/culoare.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Five cards of the same suit.

### Straight

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Straight](/images/wiki/general/chinta-clasica.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

A sequence of five consecutive cards.

### Three of a Kind

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Three of a Kind](/images/wiki/general/3lafel.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Three cards having the same rank, along with two other cards of different ranks.

### Two Pair

<figure markdown="span">
    ![Two Pair](/images/wiki/general/2perechi.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Two cards having the same rank, another two cards having the same rank (but different from the rank of the first two cards) and a fifth card of a different rank.

### One Pair

<figure markdown="span">
    ![One Pair](/images/wiki/general/1pereche.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Two cards having the same rank, along with three other cards of different ranks.

### High Card

<figure markdown="span">
    ![High Card](/images/wiki/general/cartemare.png){ width="300" }
</figure>

Any hand that does not fall into any of the above categories.

## Specific Commands

### /poker

Through this command you will be able to join the poker table.

The command is executed successfully only if you are inside the Flamingo business, near one of the poker tables.

*Syntax: /poker*
