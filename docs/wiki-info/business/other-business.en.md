The B-Zone servers have around 160 businesses each, which is why it would be difficult to classify them all. Thus, we created this page, where all other types of businesses in the game are listed.

## LSPD/LVPD/SFPD Armoury

Supplies the police with weapons, ammunition and other necessities. See photo #1.

## Phone Company

Charges for calls and written messages (SMS). The cost of an SMS is $2 and the cost of a call is $6 / 30 seconds of conversation. See photo #2.

## Car Painting and Plating Co.

Money paid for painting and registration ends up here. The price for painting and registering vehicles is $500. See photo #3.

## House Upgrade Company

Benefits from house upgrades. Any upgrade or piece of furniture has a price of $1,000. See photo #4.

## Tow Car Company

Unlike the faction with a similar name, this company handles personal towing. Towing a car is done in exchange for $200. See photo #5.

## The Los Santos Hospital

Represents the only hospital in the game and, implicitly, in Los Santos. Here, players can get rid of drug addiction for $2. See photo #6.

## LS/LV/SF Hotdog Company

The company earns from hotdogs sold through /hotdog. A hotdog costs $60. See photo #7.

## Casinos

Here you can play gambling games at machines or with other players. See photo #8.

## Farmer Business

From here you can buy the necessary items to be able to work at the Farmer system.

## Craftsman Business

From here you can rent the necessary items to be able to work at the Craftsman job.
