Businesses are locations specific to daily life, such as FastFoods or clothing stores. On the B-Zone RPG server, these are buildings that any player can buy if they have a certain amount of money required and a certain level set for that business.

Businesses also have a certain money production like houses, so every time a player enters a business, they will be charged an amount of money called entrance fee.

<p>Any player who has at least level 9 can purchase a business.</p>
- Each business has an informational text placed next to the entrance door. Looking at the informational text you will find out the following aspects about that business:
    - The business ID, located on the first line of the paragraph.
    - The informational name of the business.
    - The names of the business owners (you can see the owners using /shareholders).
    - The level you need to be able to buy the respective business.
    - The entrance fee.

Businesses of level 20 or higher are paid commissions based on their level and the importance of the business, with some having multiple types of commissions.<br>
The commission is calculated in a complex and automatic way by the server, taking into account several specific factors.

Businesses will distribute the collected money on PayDay when the amount in the vault reaches a minimum of **$1,000**, with shareholders receiving 10% of the amount per share.

## The Shares System

<figure markdown="span">
  ![#4 Info for share holders](/images/wiki/businesses/biz_info.png){ width="800" }
  <figcaption>#4 Info for share holders</figcaption>
</figure>
The shares system is a system based on buying and selling shares in a certain business. You can own a minimum of 10% of the profit from a business and a maximum of 100%, you can also own shares in multiple businesses at once. For example, you can own 10% of CNN LS, 20% of Pizza Idlewood, 40% of Clothes Store LS, until you reach the maximum of 100% that you can own. Depending on the percentage you own of a certain business, you will also receive your share of the profit, a simple example is if you own 10% of CNN LS and it produces $1,000,000 daily, you will earn $100,000 that day owning 10% of the business.

You can sell the shares of a business to another player using the [/trade] command, to sell the shares to the state for $0 you can use the [/sellshares] command.

## Specific Commands
### /bizinfo
With this command you can check as a stockholder information about the production of the shares you hold. (*See image #4*).

### /bizstatus
With this command you can check the current income from the entire biz. (*See image #3*)

### /bizwithdraw
With this command you can withdraw money from the shares you hold at the respective biz.<br>
To be able to do this you have to go to each biz where you hold shares and use the command to be able to withdraw the money. (*See image #5*).

### /sellshare
With this command you can sell a certain number of shares to the state.<br>
<span style="color: #ff0000; font-weight: bold; letter-spacing: 1px;">WARNING!</span> The money from the state does not go to you, you practically do not receive anything in return.
