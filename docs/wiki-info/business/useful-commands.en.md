On this page you will find all the control commands for a business, commands whose meaning is explained below.

To find out absolutely all the control commands for a business on the server, use the /businesshelp command.

## General characteristics

- Find out more information about businesses on the Businesses page.
- For faster execution of the /enter and /exit commands, you can use the "F" key on your keyboard.

## Specific commands

### /bizradio

Using this command, the owner of a business will be able to play a radio station or a YouTube link inside their own business. When a player enters that business, they will hear the radio station or the soundtrack from YouTube that was set by the owner.

By using the /bizradio command inside a business that does not belong to you, you will be able to control your ability to hear the audio streams inside the business.

Also, the sound playback can be stopped by the owner using this command.

### /enter

The /enter command is usually used to enter buildings, in this case, businesses. The command is executed successfully (you will be able to enter the business) only if you are close enough to the door of that business.

For faster execution, you can use the equivalent of this command, namely the "F" key on your keyboard.

### /exit

The /exit command is usually used to exit a building, in this case, a business. The command is executed successfully (you will be able to exit the business) only if you are close enough to the exit door.

For faster execution, you can use the equivalent of this command, namely the "F" key on your keyboard.

### /sellshares

Following the use of this command, you will sell a share of the business to the state, the only money you will receive being that in the business safe, if it is not empty.

### /shareholders

Following the use of this command, you will be able to see who the shareholders of a specific business are.

### /bizwithdraw

The command has the effect of withdrawing money from the business safe, money produced from the entrance fee that players pay when entering the business.

To find out how much money you have in the business safe, type the /bizwithdraw command without specifying the amount you want to withdraw from the safe.

If you want to withdraw a certain amount of money, type the command following this model: /bizwithdraw 500, will withdraw $500 from the business safe.

For correct and successful execution of the command, you must be inside the business (if it is possible to enter inside it), as close as possible to the exit door, or outside it, as close as possible to the entrance door.

### /bizinfo

To use the command you do not need to be near the business or inside it, the command being usable anywhere you are if you are the owner of a business.

By typing the command, the server will show you some details about your business:

- Business level.
- Entrance fee value.
- Total revenue.
- Minimum value for which the business can be sold.
- The percentage of shares you hold in a certain business.
- Business production based on the shares held.

### /bizstatus

Following the use of this command, you will be able to see information about the safe of a business and the minimum value at which it can be sold.

### /mybiz

To use the command you must be the owner of a rent type business (car, bike, airplane), and using this command you will be able to manage the owned vehicles and also buy new vehicles in the business if you have not reached the limit of 10 vehicles imposed by the server.

### /arenas

Using the /arenas command you can see if the arenas available on the RPG server are occupied or not.

### /help

Using the /help -> Businesses command you can find all the information presented above directly in the game.
