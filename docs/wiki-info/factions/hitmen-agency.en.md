## General Description

Hitmen Agency - Agency of assassins in direct translation.
One of the most respected factions, the family of elite players as it is called. All you have to do is fulfill the favors of some individuals, payment is made in advance, then you have to fulfill a contract. You are a simple person in the eyes of those around you, but your mind describes the qualities of the agency. A true member of this faction must first excel through mental abilities, agility and perfection. You have the chance to take a test for entry if your application is accepted by the leader. In most cases, the faction seeks as much experience as possible and a very important quality, correct writing in Romanian language.

The agency has a limited number of vehicles such as Sparrow, Buffalo. Most of the time, agency members choose the easiest way, which is using nature and technology. The 3 cities need peace, citizens need a normal life and of course the agency needs satisfied customers.

salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 1,1 + contract_value

#### Rank 2
salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 1,2 + contract_value

#### Rank 3

#### Rank 4

#### Rank 6 or Leader

#### Rank 1
salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 1,6 + contract_value

#### Rank 2
salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 1,7 + contract_value

#### Rank 3
salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 1,8 + contract_value

salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 2 + contract_value

salary = 1260 + (6 * random(15)) * 2,1 + contract_value

Note:
- **random(15)** represents a random number from 1 to 15.
- **contract_value** represents the money paid through the /contract command by the player who placed it.

## Specific Commands

### /fvr

By executing the command, all unoccupied faction vehicles will be respawned. The command is accessible from rank 5 in any faction.

### /clothes

To change your skin according to your rank, any faction member can use the /clothes command only if they are in a clothing store.

### /portable

The command connects the assassin to the agency's server, from where they can get a contract, order weapons, etc. Once you use this command, it will connect you to the agency's server and you will be able to operate different commands.

- MyContract, with this option you can see information about your target.
- GetHit, with this option you can get a contract.
- LeaveHit, with this option you can abandon the contract.
- Order, with this option you can get weapons.
- Ranks, with this option, you can see the online agency members.
- Undercover, with this option you go undercover.
- Logout, with this option, you disconnect from the agency's server.

*Note: After completing the contract, you will have a waiting period depending on the distance from which you completed the contract.*

### /ram

The command has the same effect as the /enter command, but is only used if a member of the assassin agency must enter a house to assassinate a player.

### /ftalkpower

Through this command, a member with rank 5+ can disable the faction chat for members with a rank lower than the one chosen by them.

### /fwithdrawpower

Through this command, the faction leader can set the minimum rank for withdrawing money, drugs and materials from HQ.

### /tduty

Through this command, those who are designated with the tester function by the faction leader can go on duty to be able to conduct entrance tests to the faction.

## Waiting times for taking contracts

### For completing the contract

- From over 300 meters, or if you complete the contract with Knife, you will be able to gethit immediately.
- From 250 - 299 meters, or the target commits suicide, you will be able to gethit in 5 minutes.
- From 200 - 249 meters, you will be able to gethit in 10 minutes.
- From under 199 meters, you will be able to gethit in 15 minutes.

### For abandoning the contract

The agent does not receive waiting time in the following situations handled by the server:

1. If the target goes to /sleep while the agent is chasing them.
2. If the target is imprisoned by police or admins.
3. If the target is a mafioso and participates in war.
4. If the target is in a clan and participates in clan war.
5. If the target is in the virtual world of jail.
6. If the target participates in an event.
7. If the target participates in a live.
8. If the target is in a mission.
9. If the target is in a business where an event is taking place.
10. If the target is in a Poker match or in the Poker business.
11. If the target is AFK with 0 HP.
12. If the target is logging in.
13. If the target is in Training Room.
