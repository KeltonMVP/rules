## General Description

Sons of Anarchy is a faction based on motorcycle club activities, the faction's main activity is stealing motorcycles to impose respect throughout San Andreas state.<br>
They also deal with "squeezing" impostors who dare to wear their clothes, leaving them naked and robbed.<br>
The faction has three garages where they can deliver stolen motorcycles (Los Santos garage see image #2, Las Venturas garage see image #3, San Fierro garage see image #4).<br>
Faction members must have the necessary knowledge to start a motorcycle engine without having the key (skill 4 at the Car Jacker job).<br>
Also, if they don't want to be caught and arrested, they will have to be careful around them before stealing a motorcycle, otherwise they risk being chased and imprisoned for their deed.<br>At the same time, faction members benefit from 4 weapon packages (see image #5) to have them if needed in case they are targeted by department members for the illegalities they commit.

**Note:** You will receive the **Sawn-off-Shotgun** weapon only once, meaning in one hand only, you will not receive them in both hands.

## Salary
- The payment for each delivered car is calculated according to the skill you are working on, the vehicle you steal and the days of the respective vehicle, with the following formula: 
    - **castig_car_jacker** + **vehicle_modelid** + **vehicle_days_bonus** where: 
        - **castig_car_jacker** refers to the total amount you receive working at the Car Jacker job as follows: 
            - **Skill 1**: **$978** for players without Premium Account | **$1,467$** for Premium Account players. 
            - **Skill 2**: **$1,070** for players without Premium Account | **$1,605$** for Premium Account players. 
            - **Skill 3**: **1,200$** for players without Premium Account | **$1,800$** for Premium Account players. 
            - **Skill 4**: **$1,380** for players without Premium Account | **$2,070$** for Premium Account players. 
            - **Skill 5**: **$1,659** for players without Premium Account | **$2,488$** for Premium Account players. 
        - **vehicle_modelid** - refers to the numerical ID of the model vehicle that you deliver. 
            - *<b>Ex:</b>* 411 - Infernus | 522 - NRG-500 | 541 - Bullet etc.. - <a href="https://open.mp/ro/docs/scripting/resources/vehicleid" style="text-decoration: none;">click</a> for the complete list. 
        - **vehicle_days_bonus** refers to a money bonus depending on the number of days of the vehicle you deliver as follows: 
            - Between **0 & 1500** days - the bonus is **$100** + a random amount between $0 - $99. 
            - Between **1501 & 3000** days - the bonus is **$300** + a random amount between $0 - $99. 
            - Between **3001 & 4500** days - the bonus is **$500** + a random amount between $0 - $99. 
            - Between **4501 & 6000** days - the bonus is **$700** + a random amount between $0 - $99. 
            - From **6001+** days - the bonus is **$800** + a random amount between $0 - $99.
- If you "squeeze" an impostor, you will receive money, materials and drugs (if they have money in hand / materials / drugs).
- Calculation formula for money is: **salary = faction_rank * 500**
- Calculation formula for materials is: **salary_mats = faction_rank * 1000**
- Calculation formula for drugs is: **salary_drugs = faction_rank * 3**

## Specific Commands

### /fvr
By executing the command, all unoccupied faction vehicles will be respawned. The command is accessible from rank 5 in any faction.

### /clothes
To change your skin according to your rank, any faction member can use the /clothes command only if they are in a clothing store.

### /picklock
By executing the command, you can steal a locked motorcycle.

### /extortion
By executing the command, you can "squeeze" an impostor (a civilian who has the SoA members' skin).

### /order
The command is used by members to order a certain weapon package (weapon packages are available according to the member's rank). A single parameter is used to complete the command, the weapon package ID. Note: There are a total of 4 weapon packages. The weapons contained in each package are listed in image #5.

### /ftalkpower
Through this command, a member with rank 5+ can disable the faction chat for members with a rank lower than the one chosen by them.

### /fwithdrawpower
Through this command, the faction leader can set the minimum rank for withdrawing money, drugs and materials from HQ.

### /tduty
Through this command, those who are designated with the tester function by the faction leader can go on duty to be able to conduct entrance tests to the faction.


## The Parcel System in the Laboratory
The Laboratory System is available exclusively to Sons of Anarchy members and allows the cultivation and harvesting of enhanced drugs within the faction's laboratory.

The laboratory contains 12 plots, each of which can be assigned to a single SOA member. A member can own only one plot at a time. Access to the laboratory is allowed to all members, but only eligible members can plant and harvest.

### Allocation and Eligibility of Parcels
Plots are assigned by leaders and sub-leaders via /members -> SoA Lab Control Panel.<br>
To receive a plot, a member must:

- to have rank 4+.
- not to be banned.
- to be active (inactive over 3 days = ineligible).
- not already own a plot.

Managing the rotation of plots between members is the responsibility of the management. If a member leaves the faction, his plot is automatically unassigned.

### Parcers Functionability

After assignment, only the owner can plant the plot using the **Y** key. The growth cycle lasts 24 hours, and the plot displays information about the owner and progress.

Watering can be done by any SOA member, regardless of rank, on a plot that does not belong to him.<br>
Watering reduces the growing time by 1 hour, can be performed once every 4 hours for each plot and provides 1 activity report point (with IP anti-abuse protection).<br>
Plots ready for harvest cannot be watered.

### Harvesting and Rewards

After the growth cycle is completed, only the owner can harvest the plot. This is notified at payday and at login.<br>
The amount harvested depends on the Drugs Dealer skill:

- Skill 1 - **600g**
- Skill 2 - **700g**
- Skill 3 - **800g**
- Skill 4 - **900g**
- Skill 5 - **1,000g**

At harvest, the member receives:

- 4 Clan XP.
- 20 marathon points.
- 2 skill points.
- chance for x2 skill points, depending on the skins owned.

The harvest is automatically notified to the faction.

### Withering and State of Parks

If a plot is not harvested within 12 hours after it becomes ready, it withers.<br>
The progress is completely reset, but the plot assignment remains unchanged.

On payday and logging in, owners receive updates on the status of the plot:

- Growing
- Done
- Withered

The wither is announced to the faction, and the owner is notified if he is online.
