## General Description

The daily activity of reporters is represented by writing newspapers, editing breaking news, interviewing players and, occasionally, organizing unique contests.

The News Reporters faction has earned respect through the way it handles certain situations, but also through the good upbringing of its members, who prove their seven years at home every time they have the opportunity, by supporting all players who need help.

Potential News Reporters recruits must be receptive throughout the testing period, be very attentive to behavior and the way they speak with others. Also, all applicants need, mandatory, the basic principles of the faction: respect, attention, lack of pride, the ability to write correctly from a grammatical point of view, principles without which you cannot become a News Reporters member.

## Specific Commands

### /duty

Through this command, a faction member goes on duty or off duty. *(whether they receive requests for licenses or not)*

### /fvr

By executing the command, all unoccupied faction vehicles will be respawned. The command is accessible from rank 5 in any faction.

### /clothes

To change your skin according to your rank, any faction member can use the /clothes command only if they are in a clothing store.

### /papers

Displays a list of all newspapers available at that moment, specifying the author, title, content and motto of the newspaper, including different buttons like "WRITE" to be able to write a new newspaper, "DELETE" for members with minimum rank 3+, who can delete a newspaper and "BRING" to pick up the respective newspaper so it can be delivered.

### /duty

The /duty command activates certain functions for a reporter, such as the /news command.

### /news

By using this command, a reporter sends a message that all players logged on the servers can see. There is an anti-abuse system through which you can only give 15 announcements through which you can receive money, after 15 announcements given you will no longer receive money in that hour. Also to receive salary from this command, you must give announcements in the interval 09:00 - 23:00, Romanian time.

### /live

This command is used to add or remove someone from a live interview. Messages from the interview can be seen by all server players. The command uses as parameter the player's ID or name:
*Example to add/remove a person from interview: /live Adi007*

### /endlive

This command ends a live for all participants.

### /callout

You can add a newspaper motto, next to your skin will appear a free space with a text chosen by you, by default it is "Thanks for buying my newspaper".

### /readpaper

With this command you can read the newspaper.

### /deliver

With this command reporters can sell newspapers to players, the price being between $1 and $100.

### /ftalkpower

Through this command, a member with rank 5+ can disable faction chat for members with a rank lower than the one chosen by them.

### /fwithdrawpower

Through this command, the faction leader can set the minimum rank for withdrawing money, drugs and materials from HQ.

### /tduty

Through this command, those designated with the tester function by the faction leader can go on duty to conduct faction entry tests.
