## General Description

San Fierro Taxi is located on a mountain peak, far from the hustle and bustle of the city.
San Fierro Taxi Cab Company is a taxi company that ensures safe and timely transportation anytime and anywhere.
The faction headquarters is located in the south of San Fierro city, on a hill near Mount Chilliad.

When a taxi driver uses the /fare command to activate their duty function, they will receive an information table on the right side of the screen. The table includes:

- The fare value set through the /fare command.
- Total money earned from the ride.
- Total kilometers traveled.
- The name of the taxi company you are part of.

## Salary

### For players without premium account

- **Rank 1:** - earning = total_payment * 4.4
- **Rank 2:** - earning = total_payment * 4.8
- **Rank 3:** - earning = total_payment * 5.2
- **Rank 4:** - earning = total_payment * 5.6
- **Rank 5:** - earning = total_payment * 6.0
- **Rank 6 or Leader:** - earning = total_payment * 6.4

### For players with premium account

- **Rank 1:** - earning = total_payment * 6.4
- **Rank 2:** - earning = total_payment * 6.8
- **Rank 3:** - earning = total_payment * 7.2
- **Rank 4:** - earning = total_payment * 7.6
- **Rank 5:** - earning = total_payment * 8.0
- **Rank 6 or Leader:** - earning = total_payment * 8.4

**Note:**

- *total_plata* represents the money that the taxi customer will pay.

## Specific commands

### /fvr
By executing the command, all unoccupied faction vehicles will be respawned. The command is accessible from rank 5 in any faction.

### /clothes
To change your skin according to your rank, any faction member can use the /clothes command only if they are in a clothing store.

### /fare
/fare is the command used by taxi drivers to set the fare they apply to a client. The fare is **35$** in the interval **08:00 - 20:00**, and **55$** in the interval **20:00 - 08:00**.
Due to abuse, the /fare command will no longer work on taxi company helicopters.

### /tx
With this command all three taxi companies can communicate with each other.

### /servicecalls
Through this command, taxi drivers can see unaccepted calls.

### /ftalkpower
Through this command a member with rank 5+ can disable faction chat for members with a rank lower than the one chosen by them.

### /fwithdrawpower
Through this command the faction leader can set the minimum rank for withdrawing money, drugs and materials from HQ.

### /tduty
Through this command those who are designated with the tester function by the faction leader can go on duty to be able to conduct faction entry tests.
