## Licenses & General Description

LV School Instructors can provide you with any of the following licenses for a sum of money fixed by the respective faction's regulations, which all instructors are obliged to respect:

1. Weapon (you can own a weapon)
2. Materials (you can purchase materials for the purpose of selling weapons or procured materials)
3. Fishing (allows legal fishing)
4. Flying (allows piloting an airplane/helicopter)
5. Sailing (allows navigating a boat)

*Note: The amount of money required to obtain the above licenses differs depending on the aspiring player's level.*

## Earnings Based on License Sold

1. Weapon - **$2,000**
2. Materials - **$1,600**
3. Fishing - **$500**
4. Flying - **$1,800**
5. Sailing - **$1,700**

## Level Required for License Acquisition

1. Weapon - level 5
2. Materials - level 1
3. Fishing - level 1
4. Flying - level 1
5. Sailing - level 1

**It should be mentioned that this is the minimum level required by the server; the internal regulations may not allow certain licenses from the same level.**

## NPC License Purchase or Renewal

<figure markdown="span">
  ![NPC SF](/images/wiki/general/ActorLV.png){ width="300" }
  <figcaption>#1 NPC</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure markdown="span">
  ![Dialog reinoire](/images/wiki/general/Dialog_renew.png){ width="300" }
  <figcaption>#2 Dialog for selecting the desired license and its purchase price</figcaption>
</figure>

- Next to the Training Room entrance, there is an NPC from which players can purchase or renew their desired licenses directly without having to take a test for them.
- This option is only available if the player has already attempted to request an instructor and within 5 minutes their request has not been accepted by any instructor on duty.
- If there is no instructor on duty at that moment, the license purchase dialog will be displayed directly.
- The license prices are considerably higher than usual from an instructor, depending on the player's level as follows:
    - **Flying License:**
        - Level 1-9: costs **$4,500**
        - Level 10-49: costs **$9,000**
        - Level 50+: costs **$18,000**
    - **Sailing License:**
        - Level 1-9: costs **$4,250**
        - Level 10-49: costs **$8,500**
        - Level 50+: costs **$17,000**
    - **Fishing License:**
        - Level 1-9: costs **$1,250**
        - Level 10-49: costs **$2,500**
        - Level 50+: costs **$5,000**
    - **Weapon License:**
        - Level 5-9: costs **$5,000**
        - Level 10-49: costs **$10,000**
        - Level 50+: costs **$20,000**
    - **Materials License:**
        - Level 1-9: costs **$4,000**
        - Level 10-49: costs **$8,000**
        - Level 50+: costs **$16,000**
- Once the desired license is selected from the NPC dialog (see image #2), the available hours for it are automatically set to 100.

## Specific Commands

### /duty
With this command a faction member goes on duty or off duty. (whether they receive license requests or not)

### /fvr
By executing the command, all unoccupied faction vehicles will be respawned. The command is accessible from rank 5 in any faction.

### /clothes
To change your skin according to your rank, any faction member can use the /clothes command only if they are in a clothing store.

### /sx
With this command all three instructor schools can communicate with each other.

### /startlesson
With this command an instructor starts a client's lesson to offer them a license.

### /stoplesson
Using this command an instructor stops a client's lesson.

### /givelicense
An instructor with at least rank 1 can use this command. By using it, an instructor offers a certain license to a player after taking the tests.

*Example: /givelicense Adi007 Fishing*

### /servicecalls
With this command, instructors can see unclaimed requests.

### /ftalkpower

With this command a member with rank 5+ can disable faction chat for members with a rank lower than the one they choose.

### /fwithdrawpower
With this command the faction leader can set the minimum rank for withdrawing money, drugs and materials from HQ.

### /tduty
With this command those designated as testers by the faction leader can go on duty to conduct faction entry tests.
