## General Characteristics
* The Daily Task System is a simple, linear list, a highly interactive 3x3 Grid System (Bingo-like).
* Players must form lines (horizontal, vertical or diagonal) to maximize their daily payouts.
* To access the new dynamic interface, players must use the (**/tasks**) command.
* To ensure diversity and prevent the system from feeling repetitive, a massive set of **82 unique tasks** exist in total, covering all aspects of the server (driving, working on jobs, exploring, shopping, interacting, etc.).
* These tasks are divided into 3 difficulty levels, which are strategically distributed across the 3x3 grid by the server algorithm:

## Grid Mechanics and Rewards
* Every day, the server allocates exactly 9 tasks on the player's board.
* By completing the tasks, players can form up to 8 possible lines (3 horizontal, 3 vertical, 2 diagonal).
* **Line Reward:** For each completed line, the player will instantly receive **$7,500 and 1 MP**.
* **Full Board Bonus:** If a player shows true dedication and manages to complete all 9 tasks (filling the entire board), they will trigger the final bonus, which will reward them with **$30,000, 10 MP, and 5 RP**.


## Completing the Weekly Series
* If a player successfully completes the 3x3 board every day for an entire week, they will unlock a special additional reward of **$200,000 on the 7th day**.


## Task Reroll System
* Recognizing that certain tasks may not suit a player's playstyle on a given day, a reroll feature has been added.
* The reroll action forces the server to generate a completely new set of 9 tasks from the total list of 82.
* **Cooldown & Rules**: The reroll can be made **once every 10 minutes**. However, to prevent abuse, players can only change the 9 tasks if they have not completed any lines yet that day. Once a line is formed and the reward claimed, the panel is permanently locked for the rest of the day.


## Help Menu (? Button)
* The Help Button (?) button has been integrated directly into the interface to help players track their progress without using external commands.
* **Page 1 (Overview & Tracking):**
* Displays a quick explanation of the grid mechanics and lists the player's 9 current tasks along with their real-time completion progress (e.g., 2/3 hotdogs eaten).
* Completed tasks are nicely highlighted in green with the mention (COMPLETED).
* **Page 2 (Activity Calendar):**
* Displays a fully dynamic, script-generated calendar for the current month.
* Days are color-coded (Green for fully completed panels, Orange for the current day in progress, Red/Gray for missed days).
* This visual tracker is crucial for players aiming for the $200,000 Weekly Bonus.
* **Page 3 (Daily Passive Rewards):**
* Displays a clear list of classic rewards based on hours played (3, 4, 6, and 10 hour play bonuses).
* Players can now easily check their hours played that day and see exactly what perks they will unlock at the next milestone.
