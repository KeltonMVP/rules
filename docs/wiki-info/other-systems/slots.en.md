## How to play?

* The command used to play is /spin and can only be used in the following locations in Las Venturas city: 4 Dragons or Redsands West.
* An amount of money between $1,000 and $50,000 can be inserted into the machine.
* To play, the desired amount will be inserted into the machine using the arrows and the SPIN button will be pressed.
* There are a total of 3 columns on the machine screen, along which 5 types of symbols will appear when the SPIN button is pressed: dollar sign, heart, star, skull, tiki statue.
* The MIN and MAX buttons can be used to directly reach the minimum and maximum value that can be inserted into the machine ($1,000-$50,000).

## Meaning of each symbol

Each symbol has a different importance and in case of winning, different amounts of money will be collected as follows:

* Dollar sign - 30 multiplied by the amount inserted into the machine.
* Heart - 15 multiplied by the amount inserted into the machine.
* Star - 10 multiplied by the amount inserted into the machine.
* Tiki statue - 3 multiplied by the amount inserted into the machine.
* Skull - 2 multiplied by the amount inserted into the machine.

The winnings under the graphical interface are updated according to the value inserted into the machine.


## Winning chances
* 0.19% chance of getting a line with 3 dollar signs.
* 0.76% chance of getting a line with 3 hearts
* 0.76% chance of getting a line with 3 stars
* 3.32% chance of getting a line with 3 tiki statues
* 3.32% chance of getting a line with 3 skulls


## Specific commands
### /spin
By typing the command, the machine interface will be displayed with which you can play.

## How long does it take to select the symbols and what are the winning rotations?

* The time required to complete a rotation varies between 4 and 6 seconds (depending on the random values generated by the server).
* The winning rotation is considered when all 3 symbols of the same type are displayed on the middle line of the machine (for example heart-heart-heart).
