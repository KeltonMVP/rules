The Gold Award system encourages all RPG server players to be as active as possible, the system takes into account *only* hours played actively, each second played is counted to receive this bonus. Hours played from */sleep* are not taken into consideration, only hours actually played are taken into account.


## General Characteristics
* You can see at any time the top of the most active players of that month using the */goldaward* command
* To receive the amount of 1000 Gold you will have to be the most active player of that month.
* The rest of the players who are in 2nd place or lower will not be rewarded.
* You can also see your hours for */goldaward* on */stats*.
