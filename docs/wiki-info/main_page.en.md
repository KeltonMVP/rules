<h3 align="center">About B-Zone Community</h3>
<p>The B-Zone community currently owns a single SA-MP server, enjoying a wide audience both nationally and internationally. The community's RPG server is aimed at all lovers of the game San Andreas, offering them an online experience with friends. Our players benefit from a unique Gamemode, with numerous systems that have as their main purpose fun, which is frequently improved.</p>
<p>The first server was established on <b>September 1, 2008</b> and has been growing in popularity ever since. Since then, we have established 3 other servers running the same Gamemode and enjoying similar success, which merged in August 2019 as RPG1 (<b>RPG1 and RPG3</b>) and RPG2 (<b>RPG2 and RPG4</b>), these also enjoy a resounding success, with a high number of players active on our servers.</p>
<p>Subsequently, these two servers (<b>RPG1 & RPG2</b>) also merged with each other on <b>July 1, 2023</b>, forming a single community server with a large number of players.</p>
