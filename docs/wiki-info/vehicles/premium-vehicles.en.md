## Legend

* Dealership Price - The price for which you can buy a new vehicle from the Dealership.
* Dealership Refund - The amount of money you receive if you sell your car to the Dealership (/v -> Sell).
* Maximum speed - The maximum speed the vehicle can reach.
* Inspired from - Names of real cars associated by similarity with those in San Andreas.
* Number of seats - The maximum number of players that can enter the vehicle at the same time (including the driver).
* Price in Gold - Represents the amount of gold needed to purchase the vehicle.
* Tuneable - If the vehicle is tuneable, the name of the tuning shop to which it belongs is displayed, otherwise the message "Not tuneable" is displayed.
