# Cash Vehicles

- Standard (cash) vehicles on RPG servers are vehicles that can be purchased using in-game currency ($) starting from level 3.
- Standard (cash) vehicles can be sold to other players using the /trade command. They can also be sold to the Dealership for 60% of their value.

## Legend

- Dealership Price - The price at which you can buy a new vehicle from the Dealership.
- Dealership Refund - The amount of money you receive if you sell your car to the Dealership (/v -> vehicle -> '''Sell''').
- Dealership Gold Refund - The amount of money you receive if you sell your car to the Dealership (/v -> vehicle -> '''Sell'''), when it was purchased with Gold.
- Maximum Speed - The maximum speed the vehicle can reach.
- Based on - Real car names associated by similarity with those in San Andreas.
- Number of Seats - The maximum number of players that can enter the vehicle at the same time (including the driver).
- Price in Gold - If it appears in a vehicle's information list, it means that the vehicle can be purchased using real currency, gold (so it can also be found on the gold vehicles list) and displays the amount of gold required for purchase.
- Tunable - If the vehicle is tunable, the name of the tuning shop it belongs to is displayed, otherwise the message "Not tunable" is shown.
