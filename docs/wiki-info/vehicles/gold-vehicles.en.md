# Gold Vehicles

- Below you will find a list of all B-Zone RPG server vehicles that can be purchased from the Dealership using Gold.
- Vehicles purchased with Gold from the Dealership cannot be sold to other players, only returned to the Dealership.
- The refund value for vehicles purchased with Gold is noted for each vehicle in the list below and represents 40% of their value.
- You do not need a premium account if you choose to buy such a vehicle, only for premium vehicles.

## Legend

- Dealership Price (Gold) - The price of the item purchased with Gold in the Dealership from the shop.
- Dealership Gold Refund - The amount of money you receive if you sell your car to the Dealership (/v -> vehicle -> sell).
- Maximum Speed - The maximum speed the vehicle can reach.
- Based on - Real car names associated by similarity with those in San Andreas.
- Number of Seats - The maximum number of players that can enter the vehicle at the same time (including the driver).
- Price in Gold - Represents the amount of Gold required to purchase the vehicle.
- Tunable - If the vehicle is tunable, the name of the tuning shop it belongs to is displayed, otherwise the message "Not tunable" is shown.
