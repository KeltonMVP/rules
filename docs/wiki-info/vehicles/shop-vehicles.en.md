# Shop Vehicles

- Vehicles are unique on the server and can only be purchased through a [ticket on the website](https://www.rpg.b-zone.ro/ticket).
- Vehicles cannot be sold to players, and they can be sold to the Dealership for $0.
- Vehicles cannot be purchased through the Dealership, only through the methods presented above.
- The Gold for purchasing such a vehicle must have been ordered from the shop in the last 7 days.
- The complete list of all vehicles that can be purchased:
